Paid Media Brief

The job

Prepare Facebook and Google Search campaigns to a ready-to-launch state, distilled from paid-media-plan.html — without activating spend. Adam Hall Buy My Car already converts roughly 25 cars a month on trust alone with zero paid-media history; the constraint is capacity and measurement, not demand. This channel activates only once all three Now-to-Next triggers hold together: at least one full month of usable lead-log data, CQ11/CQ06/CQ08 answered, and a second-buyer conversation under way — and only once the website rebuild (website-brief.html) is live with analytics and call tracking. Under the Conservative budget scenario, paid spend stays at zero indefinitely.

Audience

Two audiences only, both geo-fenced strictly to Gold Coast, Brisbane and Northern Rivers postcodes: downsizers aged 55+ (Facebook only) and estate/health-crisis adult-child decision-makers aged 35–55 (Facebook and Google Search — "sell my father's car"-type search intent is a confirmed low-competition, high-relevance cluster). No lookalike, interest-based expansion or behavioural targeting until real conversion data exists. Full detail in customer-profile.html.

The message

Core promise: "Free, obligation-free, no catch." Support points: a real person, not a call centre or app; comes to you; explains the number. Call to action: "Call and find out — it costs you nothing to ask" — never "book now," which overstates readiness at the Exposure/Consideration stage this ad sits at. Voice: plain, unhurried, never urgency-led (copy-deck.html). Creative must never borrow the "act now" register used by CarBuyers or We Buy Cars.

Constraints

Conservative scenario: $0, paid channels off entirely. Moderate scenario (Next stage onward only): Facebook approximately 40% of the paid test budget, Google Ads approximately 25% — illustrative percentages only, never a fixed dollar figure, pending CQ18. No target CPA or ROAS is set; the first full test month sets the working baseline. Manual bidding only for the entire first test month on both platforms — no automated bid strategy against zero historical data. Day-parting must match Adam's actual availability to take a call, never run 24/7 by default. Call tracking is mandatory before any spend goes live. Facebook and Google Search only — TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, X, Snapchat, Google Display, Performance Max, Shopping, YouTube paid ads and any broad-reach programmatic are explicitly out of scope this phase.

Success and reporting

Primary KPIs: cost per click-through (Facebook); cost per booked home visit (both platforms); tracked phone-call conversions. Test one variable at a time — message, then audience, then creative — never simultaneously. A minimum of one full calendar month before judging performance; a minimum of a handful of tracked conversions before considering Google's Maximise Conversions. Reported monthly, folded into the same report as every other channel; escalate to Adam if cost per booked visit trends toward a level that only makes sense against a materially higher average retail gross than the confirmed ~$4,200 baseline.

First two weeks (once the activation trigger clears)

  1. Confirm with Adam and Maxxim jointly that the Now-to-Next trigger conditions have genuinely cleared — a date alone is not the trigger.
  2. Set up both ad accounts using the naming convention above; confirm the Meta Pixel and Google Ads conversion tag are firing on the live site.
  3. Confirm the dedicated tracked phone number is live and attributing correctly before any spend starts.
  4. Launch both campaigns at the minimum viable daily budget, single message, single audience per platform.
  5. Run a weekly, not monthly, review cadence for the first month, until a full month of trustworthy data exists.

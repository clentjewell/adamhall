SEO Brief

The job

Take the rebuilt site (see website-brief.html) from zero organic-search structuring to a fully indexed, schema-marked, locally optimised site within three months of launch, then build page-one visibility on the two open, low-competition clusters — Northern Rivers regional terms and estate/health-crisis terms — within six months. Distilled from seo-strategy.html. There is no existing keyword ranking, backlink or analytics baseline: this is a build from zero, not an optimisation of a running programme.

Audience

Two search audiences: downsizers aged 60–75+ across Gold Coast, Brisbane and Northern Rivers searching plain, local, transactional terms ("sell my car gold coast"); and adult-child decision-makers researching quietly, increasingly via an AI assistant rather than a keyword search ("how do I sell my father's car"). Both are detailed in customer-profile.html.

The message

Core promise: a free, obligation-free home valuation from a real, known local person — never a platform or a branch network. Support points: 27+ years of trade experience; a decade of public, on-air valuation credibility; three-region local coverage including Northern Rivers, a region no researched competitor visibly serves. Voice: plain, everyday language, never corporate or legalistic (copy-deck.html).

Constraints

Budget is scenario-scaled, no fixed dollar figure confirmed (CQ18) — technical foundation work is near-zero-cost and proceeds regardless of scenario; a dedicated keyword-research/backlink tool is a Moderate-scenario line only. Every keyword-difficulty rating in seo-strategy.html section 3 is a directional estimate, not a data-backed figure — never present it as verified data. No milestone here promises a page-one ranking on a fixed date. The "one number, never moved" guarantee must not appear in any published SEO content until CQ01–CQ04 are confirmed by Adam. Review schema is deferred until a genuine public review platform exists — never fabricate a review count or rating. CQ11 (brand naming) is not decided here; every page is built to hold under the current name.

Success and reporting

Three months: site live and indexed, sitemap submitted, schema verified, Search Console and analytics reporting, a ranking baseline established. Six months: page-one presence on the Northern Rivers and estate/health-crisis clusters; organic-attributed calls and callbacks appearing in the monthly report for the first time. Twelve months: building visibility on Gold Coast and Brisbane head terms; a public review presence established. Organic sessions and rankings are leading indicators only — the metric that matters is website-attributed leads rolling into the lead-source-split metric in measurement-plan.html. Reported monthly, folded into the same report as every other channel.

How to read this document

This is the full page tree for the rebuilt site — every page, its single job, and its primary keyword or topic, kept consistent with seo-strategy.html. The reasoning behind this shape lives in website-strategy.html; the per-page content plans for the pages that matter most live in page-strategy.html. Fifteen pages in total, one level deep at most — flat, because this is a single-core-service, phone-first business with no product catalogue to nest.

Navigation

Main navigation, in order: Home; How It Works; Sell Your Car (with Gold Coast, Brisbane and Northern Rivers listed beneath it); Selling a Parent's or Family Member's Car; What's Your Car Worth?; Adam's Story; Testimonials; FAQ; Contact. The phone number is not a nav item — it is a persistent header element and, on mobile, a sticky footer button, visible regardless of which nav item is open, per website-strategy.html's conversion strategy.

Footer contents. One NAP block (business name, phone 0404 290 617, service area — Gold Coast, Brisbane and Northern Rivers); a quick-links column (How It Works, FAQ, Testimonials, Refer a Friend); a legal column (Privacy Policy, Terms of Use); a short radio-credit line ("Hear Adam on 4CRB 89.3FM — What's Your Car Worth?"); copyright line. The footer's NAP block is the single source other on-page instances (schema, header, contact page) must match exactly — never hand-typed differently in more than one place.

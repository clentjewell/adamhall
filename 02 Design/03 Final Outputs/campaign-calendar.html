Campaign Calendar

How to read this document

This is the year at a glance — the operating rhythm the campaign framework in marketing-plan.html runs on, laid out August 2026 to July 2027 (twelve months from this document's date). It is consistent with, and does not repeat, the campaign detail already given in marketing-plan.html — objectives, channels, target segments and full KPI detail live there; this document is the timeline.

Seasonality is a hypothesis here, not a confirmed pattern. buying-triggers-barriers.html states plainly that no seasonal pattern in this business's demand has been confirmed with Adam — it is explicitly flagged "NOT AVAILABLE, ask client" in the underlying discovery dataset. The quarterly themes below are anchored to genuine, real Australian moments (financial-year timing, recognised seniors' calendar events, the Australian retail/community calendar) as plausible windows to test — not to demand patterns this business has proven. Confirm actual seasonal volume patterns with Adam once the lead log has run long enough to show one, and adjust this calendar accordingly rather than treating it as fixed.

Every campaign entry below is sequenced behind the capacity-and-measurement-first rule in marketing-plan.html and strategic-priorities.html — nothing in Quarter 1 is a demand-generation push.

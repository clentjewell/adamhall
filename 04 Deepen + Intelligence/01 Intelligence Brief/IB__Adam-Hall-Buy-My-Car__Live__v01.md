## Snapshot – one-line commercial read

The constraint is not demand or trust; it is that the entire conversion system lives in one person's head, which makes the business unhireable-into, capacity-capped and unsellable.

## Current State (Confirmed)

Adam Hall Buy My Car turns over approximately $5M a year. It buys around 25 cars a month (range 20–30), split roughly 90% retail and 10% wholesale, at an average retail gross of about $4,200 per car. Customers are mostly 60–75 and older – downsizers, licence-loss sellers, and estate or health-driven sellers – who choose this business on trust first and price second.

This is a trust-conversion business operating inside a car-buying shell, not a car business that happens to be trusted. The near-term target is $7M, with a longer 10x path toward a named, trusted, sellable buying brand. The north star is Cars For Us: founded 2019, grew to $500M, sold to Toyota for approximately $120M.

## The Constraint

Adam is the system. Valuation logic, yes/no instinct, customer-trust technique and payment/settlement discipline live only in his head, undocumented. Every transaction depends on Adam personally reading the seller, setting the number and calling the decision. Nothing about how he does this is written down, tested or transferable.

## Cultural Spine – the Adam Hall Standard

Empathy first then the solution. Daily self-criticism. Ownership not excuses. Stay in the room. Value your time commercially. The business hires and trains for these values first, skills second. George is the cautionary case of what happens when that order reverses – skill hired before values.

## Scope Agreed (15 July intro)

Six workstreams are agreed as the shape of the engagement:

1. Strategy – the mapping and rebuild of Adam's judgement into a documented operating system.
2. Branding update – a brand book revamp. The existing branding is sound; it needs a tickle-up, not a rebuild.
3. E-commerce marketplace website – built to be visible to AI chatbots and generative search, not just traditional search.
4. Vehicle inspection/input tool – modelled on the Jewell Tyres field tool, switching between an internal cost view and a customer-facing report, feeding both the inventory system and the website marketplace.
5. Marketing activation – socials, EDMs, and the full activation plan.
6. AI operating system portal – an "ask it anything" brain for the business and team, built on the documented judgement captured through mapping.

## High-Control Register

Anything touching money, customer ID, finance owing (PPSR), ownership, rego transfer, payment or settlement paperwork is HIGH-CONTROL. These items are confirmed with Adam directly, never assumed or invented. Trust moments – home visits, reassuring a grieving seller – are documented to train people, never to replace them.

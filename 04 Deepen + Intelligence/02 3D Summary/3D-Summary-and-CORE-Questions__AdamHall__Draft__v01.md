## The Engagement on a Page

Adam Hall Buy My Car is a trust-conversion business, not a car business. It turns over roughly $5M buying ~25 cars a month at ~$4,200 retail gross, 90% retail, for customers who are mostly 60–75+ and choose on trust before price. The constraint is not demand and it is not trust. It is that the entire conversion system – valuation logic, yes/no instinct, trust technique, settlement discipline – lives in one head, which makes the business unhireable-into, capacity-capped and unsellable.

The engagement extracts that judgement and rebuilds the business around it in three phases. Re-Discover maps how Adam actually runs it, live, two sessions a week. Design turns the mapping into the Adam Hall Operating System: SOPs, valuation rules, scripts, a values-first hiring brief and the brand architecture. Deploy activates it: the team runs the system, the brand goes public, marketing moves to always-on. JewellAI keeps the Operating System current as sessions bank new judgement. Maxxim runs the marketing system. The same method is live for BTC. The north star is Cars For Us: 2019 to $500M to a $120M sale to Toyota.

## Running the Session

Ask in Adam's language, one question at a time, and take the last real example when an answer goes vague. CQ04 and CQ21 sit in the high-control register – money, ID, PPSR, ownership, rego, settlement – so capture those sequences precisely and confirm them back to Adam in writing before anything is built on them. Automation ideas that surface go to the backlog, not into the room.

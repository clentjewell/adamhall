This is KH00, the Knowledge Hub Index and master front door to everything Jewell Projects and Adam Hall Buy My Car know about this engagement. It is organised on the Jewell OS 3D Process structure – Re-Discover, Design, Activate – so any artefact can be found, named and trusted. Start here before searching anywhere else in the repo.

## How this repo is organised

The repo follows the standard Jewell OS folder taxonomy:

- **00 Admin** – commercial material: meeting notes, proposals, pricing, and the Artifact Registry that tracks every output.
- **01 Discover** – Phase 1 of the 3D Process, split into working drafts and final outputs.
- **02 Design** – Phase 2, same split: working drafts feed final outputs.
- **03 Deploy** – Phase 3, same split, covering launch and activation.
- **04 Deepen + Intelligence** – the living layer: the Intelligence Brief (IB), this index (KH00), and the 3D Summary of CORE questions. These stay current in place rather than being versioned as one-off drafts.
- **05 Blueprint Extraction** – Jewell Projects internal IP: the SOPs and orchestration logic behind how the engagement runs.
- **06 Source + Working + Imports** – raw inputs, imports and working files that feed the phase folders.
- **meetings** – raw transcripts, unedited.

Within each phase folder (01–03), documents promote upward: draft material starts in working drafts and, once approved, moves to final outputs. Only final outputs are canonical. A document sitting in a working-drafts folder is not yet the source of truth, no matter how developed it looks.

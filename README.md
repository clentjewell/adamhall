## What this engagement is

A ~$5M trust-conversion used-car business (~25 cars/month, ~90% retail, ~$4,200 average retail gross) where the entire conversion system — valuation logic, yes/no instinct, trust technique, settlement discipline — lives in one person's head. The job is to extract that judgement, document it as the Adam Hall Operating System, and rebuild the brand, sales, marketing and activation around it, so the business can hire well, scale toward $7M and beyond, and become sellable. North star: Cars For Us (2019 → $500M → sold to Toyota).

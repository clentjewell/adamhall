Undocumented, you can't hire into it with confidence, can't scale past where you are, and can't sell it — because there's nothing to sell except you.

06 — Stage 1 · The CORE

22 questions that unlock everything else.

These are the questions that turn instinct into a system. Your answers, in your words, lock the parameters everything downstream depends on. A few of them:

01Standing in a driveway, what do you weigh — in order — before you say a number?
02Think of the last car you walked away from. What flipped your yes to a no?
03Walk me through the money: agreed price to cash paid and clean title.
04If you sold this in five years, what does "sellable" look like in numbers?

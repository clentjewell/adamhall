## How to use this document

This is the **slide-by-slide copy** for the Adam Hall proposal, built to go live as a
web presentation on Cloudflare Pages (same delivery as the Jewell Tyres reference build).
Each slide below carries: a **section label**, the **on-screen copy** (headline, subhead,
body, and any stat callouts), and a short **build note** for the person assembling the deck.

Voice: Adam's own — direct, warm, no sales pressure, plain language. Short lines. Big
numbers. Nothing on a slide that Adam wouldn't say out loud. Everything commercial is
confirmed; anything still a hypothesis is not stated as fact.

**Figures are locked from the March baseline and the 15 July intro** (~$5M, ~25 cars/month,
90/10 retail split, ~$4,200 gross). Stage 2 and Stage 3 dollar figures are quoted at the
gates, not on these slides.

## Slide 08 — Stage 2: Design

**Eyebrow:** Stage 2

**Headline:** Turn the CORE into a business that runs.

**Body:** Stage 2 turns your answers into a working strategy — a full Sales, Marketing, Brand and Activation plan — plus the tools to run it on.

**Deliverables (list):**
- A **brand book revamp** — a tickle-up, not a rebuild. It's already sound.
- A **website sitemap and build plan** — the blueprint for the site.
- A **CRM and vehicle input tool** — capture a car's details in the field, switch between your cost view and a customer-facing report, feed it straight to inventory and the marketplace.
- The **Adam Hall Operating System portal** — the online home for your SOPs, scripts and rules, kept current by JewellAI.

**Plain-truth callout:** The website build itself isn't in Stage 2. What you need is an online marketplace to sell cars — that's a bigger job, scoped and priced separately once the plan exists to scope it against.

**Investment line:** Quoted at Gate 1, once Stage 1 is signed off — with your $5,000 credited against it.

> **Build note:** The input-tool bullet can link to the working reference build (Jewell Tyres inspection tool). Keep the "website not included" callout visually distinct — honesty is the sell here.

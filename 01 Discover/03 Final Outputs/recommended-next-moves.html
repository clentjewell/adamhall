How to read this document

This is the bridge from Discover into Design — the order to tackle the priorities in priority-problems.html, the calls Adam needs to make at sign-off, and what to start moving on immediately, before Design formally begins.

Recommended sequence

  1. Close the CORE mapping sessions (CQ01–CQ22), especially CQ11, CQ06 and CQ08, before Design-phase brand and website work locks in. Reasoning: almost every downstream Design decision — naming, channel priority, sale-readiness targets — depends on these answers; sequencing brand or website work ahead of them risks costly rework (see problem 5 in priority-problems.html).
  2. Stand up basic measurement in parallel — lead log, source tagging, hours-per-deal. Reasoning: this does not depend on any CORE answer, is the lowest-cost, highest-value first step, and is the precondition for every key result in objectives-key-results.html.

  4. Only then begin Design-phase brand, positioning and website-strategy work, informed by validated answers rather than the hypotheses this Discover phase has necessarily relied on.

Objectives & Key Results

How to read this document

Four objectives turn success-definition.html into an accountable plan for the engagement horizon. Because untracked metrics — conversion rates, lead volumes, Adam-hours per deal — sit at the centre of the diagnosis in funnel-review.html and sales-process-review.html, Objective 1 is deliberately about measuring before optimising: no other objective's key results can be judged against a baseline until this one is live. Baselines are marked TBD — baseline needed everywhere the discovery data confirms nothing is currently tracked.
